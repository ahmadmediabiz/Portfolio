T-Life Hostel

Travel Video / Taichung, Taiwan

Background

This video was created to give a glimpse of Taichung, Taiwan, along with life at T-Life Hostel. It includes points of interest in Taichung and shots of the city and hostel atmosphere.

Process

I went around the city capturing the atmosphere with my camera, then edited the footage with fast-paced music to get potential visitors excited about the city and the hostel.

Results

A minute-long travel video that depicts the fun vibes of T-Life Hostel and the city of Taichung.
