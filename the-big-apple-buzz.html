The Big Apple Buzz

Travel Video / New York City

Background

This video was made for anyone who wanted a quick way to learn about New York City so they could plan a trip and get a better idea of what they might experience.

Process

I styled the edit around the fact that New York City can be busy and frantic, using music and visually pleasing fast cuts. All footage used was stock footage.

Results

A minute-long travel video of NYC that can be viewed on YouTube.
