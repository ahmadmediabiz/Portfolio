About Me

Lansing, Michigan / Sony A7IV

Hi! My name is Muhammad Ahmad, and I am a Content Creator, Digital Marketer, and Photographer based in Lansing, Michigan. I mainly shoot with my Sony A7IV.

I've been making content for social media sites ever since I was eleven years old, and have slowly evolved into the YouTube vlogs and TikToks that I make today of my life, whether I'm traveling, working out, or cooking.

I graduated from Arizona State University in May 2025 with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Information Technology. Combining marketing experience with content creation helps me understand what it takes to push an idea or story forward.
