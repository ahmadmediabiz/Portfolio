East Lansing Travel Video

Travel Video / East Lansing

Background

This video was created to give a glimpse of the city of East Lansing, including different forms of transportation and points of interest.

Process

I ran around the city capturing the atmosphere with my mirrorless camera and my drone, then edited the footage with calm music to match the vibe of the city.

Results

A minute-long travel video that depicts everyday life, the overall atmosphere, and things to do in East Lansing.
